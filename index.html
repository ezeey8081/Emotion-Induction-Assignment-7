Emotion Induction Assignment

Instructions

Complete the chart below for each emotion by:

  1. Describing a specific behavior or situation from others that typically triggers this emotion in you
  2. Rating the intensity of the emotional response (1 = Mild, 5 = Strong)
  3. Describing your typical behavioral reaction
  4. Stating what you wish the other person had done instead

After completing the chart, write a reflection (approximately 300 words) on what you learned.

Deliverable 2: Reflection

After completing the spreadsheet, write a brief essay that explores what you learned (approximately 300 words).

Guiding Questions:

  • What surprised you about how certain behaviors from others affect you emotionally?
  • Are there recurring themes or patterns in what tends to trigger strong emotions?
  • How might greater awareness of these triggers help you respond differently in leadership or group settings?
  • Is there one relationship in your life where this insight could make a difference?
  • How might this awareness change how you manage or lead others—especially under pressure, when giving feedback, or when receiving criticism?
  • What's one specific thing you will do differently this week based on what you've learned?
